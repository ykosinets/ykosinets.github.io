Marine & freshwater science

Exploring waters and the life within them.

Beamiie is a research group focused on understanding marine and freshwater environments. We study oceans, lakes, water systems, ecosystems, and the changing relationship between people and aquatic environments.

Research with purpose

We combine observation, data, and environmental science to support healthier waters and better knowledge of aquatic ecosystems.
